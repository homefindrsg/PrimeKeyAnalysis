The Core Methodology

The 8 Factors Used to Evaluate Investment Potential

PrimeKey Analysis evaluates residential projects across eight empirically validated factors proven to influence capital growth, rental yield, and resale liquidity.

01

Distance to MRT

Measures walkability to mass transit (< 5 mins being optimal). Directly impacts daily convenience, rental vacancy rates, and price resilience.

Impact: High Rental & Resale Liquidity
02

URA Growth Hotspots

Assesses proximity to major urban transformation zones (e.g., Jurong Lake District, Lentor). Drives infrastructure-led appreciation.

Impact: Long-Term Capital Growth
03

GLS Pipeline

Tracks nearby government land sales. Higher land costs at new land tenders create fresh pricing benchmarks that elevate existing project values.

Impact: Price Benchmark Support
04

Development Size

Evaluates unit count (400+ units optimal). Scale provides extensive facilities, lower maintenance fees, and transaction volume that anchors bank valuations.

Impact: Liquidity & Valuation Support
05

Remaining Lease Tenure

Monitors remaining lease runway on 99-year leaseholds. Avoids steep depreciation curves (Bala's Curve) and CPF financing restrictions.

Impact: Capital Protection & Financing
06

Rental Yield & Demand

Analyzes localized rental yields and tenant demand. Strong rental yields provide holding power and defensive cashflow during downturns.

Impact: Cashflow & Holding Resilience
07

Proximity to Primary Schools

Evaluates distance to sought-after primary schools (< 1km). Priority admission rules trigger perpetual family buyer/tenant demand.

Impact: Family Buyer Pool Defense
08

HDB MOP Upgrader Pool

Evaluates surrounding public flats reaching 5-year Minimum Occupation Period. A large MOP cluster provides a captive local buyer pool.

Impact: Guaranteed Secondary Demand
